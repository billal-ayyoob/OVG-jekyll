Our Company History

Occam Vision Group was founded in Boulder, CO in 2010 to combine state of the art computer vision technology with low-cost hardware.  Occam Vision Group is a DBA for Occam Robotics, Inc.

We build unique computer vision products and systems that are used for a range of robotics, automation, telepresence, tracking, and surveillance applications. We also offer turn-key OEM design services for camera hardware/software systems, computer vision algorithm development, and other high-end software and firmware projects.

Our technology acts as a bridge between real life events and high technology products, making products and services smarter.  More efficient.  More effective.
