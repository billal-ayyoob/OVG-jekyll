Mounting the Omni 60 to a standard tripod

Last Updated: 6 years ago in Setup

This article gives a brief walkthrough of how to mount the Omni 60 on a tripod. Standard tripods all include a 1/4″-20 threaded bolt extruding from a removable piece that latches onto the tripod. You can mount the Omni camera directly to the latching piece via the mounting hole that is located on the bottom of the camera.

The base of the Omni 60 has a threaded 1/4″-20 hole plugs in any standard tripod. You’ll want to remove the latching piece of the tripod top and place it gently at the base of the camera. Then rotate the threaded rod at the back of the latching piece, instead of rotating it directly.

Rotating the latching part will also work, but you’ll end of scratching the base of the camera pretty quickly as you tighten it.

Then re-latch the piece back onto the tripod and you’re done.
